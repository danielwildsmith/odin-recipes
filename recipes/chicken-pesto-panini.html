Chicken Pesto Panini

Description

A simple, fast and very tasty change from your typical sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 focaccia bread (2 inches thick), cut into fourths
  • 0.5 cup basil pesto
  • 1 cup cubed cooked chicken breast
  • 0.5 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped red onion
  • 4 slices (1 oz each) provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Heat closed contact grill 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Cut each piece focaccia in half horizontally. Spread 1 tablespoon pesto on cut sides of each half. Layer bottom halves with one-fourth of the chicken, bell pepper, onion and cheese. Cover with remaining focaccia halves.
  3. When grill is heated, place sandwiches on grill. Close grill; grill 3to 5 minutes or until focaccia is golden brown and cheese is melted.
